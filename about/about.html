About Food Blog

I launched Food Blog back in 2011 and over the years have learned how powerful food can be when we hold a positive place for it in our lives. While I was in college I developed an unhealthy relationship with food and exercise in order to avoid facing some deep-rooted hardships from my past. What started as an extra workout during the week quickly turned into counting every calorie, labeling certain foods as “good” and “bad,” and eventually lead to my battle with an eating disorder.

What you will at Food Blog

A little bit of everything – from food to fitness to my latest obsessions. Most of the recipes I create are geared towards those with active, healthy lifestyles. You’ll find plenty of good-for-you meals to share with your family and friends, and recipes the love of your life will adore. I love to get creative using unique ingredients and pairing different flavor combinations. I also enjoy baking — mostly paleo or grain free desserts, but also believe that cookies made with brown butter and real sugar can be life changing.
